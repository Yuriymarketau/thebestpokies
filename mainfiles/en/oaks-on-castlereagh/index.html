Suites Overview

Conveniently located in the heart of Sydney’s vibrant Chinatown

Whether you are in town for a weekend getaway or travelling to the CBD for business, Oaks Sydney Castlereagh Suites is centrally located in Haymarket and is in walking distance to Darling Harbour and central Station. Offering various self-contained apartment options with inclusive Wi-Fi, onsite gym, swimming pool, room service, powered by Deliveroo and TV with streaming capabilities.

Address: 317 Castlereagh Street, Haymarket, Sydney NSW 2000

Reservations: 1300 080 292

What's Around?

Oaks Sydney Castlereagh Suites seamlessly connects guests with the city and Sydneys iconic sights, experiences and attractions, the perfect base for a family getaway, business stay or romantic weekend away.

Oaks Sydney Castlereagh Suites

Located within walking distance from Sydney's Central Station, Capitol Theatre, Chinatown and Pitt Street Mall, Oaks Sydney Castlereagh Suites is a short 30 minute car, taxi or train ride away from the airport. Onsite parking is available for a fee, please call the hotel to book a spot.

What's Around?

Our property is within easy walking distance from Central Station and Museum Station and just 3 minutes walk from Sydneys historic Capitol Theatre. Close to Sydney's other iconic attractions including Chinatown, Pitt Street Mall, Paddys Markets, Sydney Aquarium, Hyde Park and the splendid Star Casino, Oaks Sydney Castlereagh Suites, is the perfect base to explore from.
